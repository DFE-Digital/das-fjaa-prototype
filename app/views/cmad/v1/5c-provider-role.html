Your training provider's responsibilities Step 3 of 3

The roles and responsibilities set out what is expected of your training provider.

They have:

  • a copy of the apprenticeship agreement signed by you and your flexi-job apprenticeship agency (FJAA)
  • discussed your training plan with you and your FJAA and ensured it accounts for prior learning, English and maths needs and additional learning support
  • told you and your FJAA your apprenticeship training start and end dates
  • explained how off-the-job training will be delivered
  • told you and your FJAA if they will use sub-contracted training providers to deliver any of your training
  • explained that you will be assessed by an end-point assessment organisation

They will:

  • manage the quality and delivery of your off-the-job training and making sure it is logged
  • take responsibility for regular progress reviews between all parties (usually every 12 weeks)
  • make sure that you and your FJAA know how to raise concerns or complaints about the apprenticeship training
