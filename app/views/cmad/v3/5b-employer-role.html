Your employer's responsibilities Step 2 of 3

The roles and responsibilities set out what is expected of CoTrain.

They have:

  • signed and provided you with a copy of your apprenticeship agreement that you agreed together
  • contributed to a training plan with you and your training provider
  • committed to working with you and your training provider to support your learning needs

They will:

  • find placements to enable you to complete all or part of your apprenticeship training
  • enable you to complete your off-the-job training during your normal working hours, which will be at least 20% of your working hours
  • take part in regular progress reviews with you and your training provider every 8 to 12 weeks
  • work with you and your training provider to resolve any concerns or complaints
  • carry out an end of employment review when your job ends
