Things you need to know

CoTrain is a flexi-job apprenticeship agency and they will pay your wages. As an agency, they will arrange placements with organisations to complete your apprenticeship training and assessment.

You may have several placements during your apprenticeship.

Before starting your apprenticeship CoTrain should have given you a:

  • copy of your apprenticeship agreement signed by you and them, if you have not received this agreement please speak to CoTrain
  • day-to-day contact or manager who will support you through your apprenticeship
